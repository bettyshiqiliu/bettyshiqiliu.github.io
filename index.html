Research Interest

I am broadly interested in the machine learning field. I am focusing on machine learning algorithms in two exciting directions: (1) building learning algorithm under the circumstance where only limited amount of data is available. (2) building learning algorithm which can generalize over tasks. These exciting areas includes multi-task learning, meta learning, unsupervised or semi-supervised learning, data augmentation. I am also interested in the theoretical properties of these learning algorithms.
